# Actions And Stuff Texture Pack

**Actions And Stuff** is a flexible Minecraft texture pack designed to enhance your gameplay with smooth animations and customizable visuals. Whether you want subtle animations or advanced shader-ready textures, this pack has you covered.

---

## Using the Pack with Other Resource Packs

This texture pack supports flexible customization thanks to its different versions.

### Available Variants
- **16x**: Full textures + animations  
- **32x**: Keep your current textures, add animations  
- **64x**: For advanced visuals and shader-friendly environments  

### Layering Tips
- Place **Actions And Stuff 1.8** at the top of your resource list when combining with other packs.  
- Avoid stacking too many animation packs to prevent conflicts.

---

## Best Settings & Recommended Configurations

To get the smoothest animation experience, try adjusting your settings based on your system and preferences.

### Recommended Settings
- **Render Distance:** 8–12 chunks  
- **Smooth Lighting:** ON  
- **Particles:** Minimal / Default  
- **FOV:** 75–80  

### For Low-End Devices
- **Render Distance:** 6–8 chunks  
- **Beautiful Skies:** OFF  
- **Simulation Distance:** 4–6  

### For Better Visuals
- **Render Distance:** 12–16 chunks  
- **Fancy Graphics:** ON  
- **Particles:** Default or High  

These configurations aim to balance smooth performance with improved visual quality.
